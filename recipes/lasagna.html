How to Make Lasagna

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below. The ingredients you will need are:

Ingredients

  • 1lb sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4lb of ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • crushed tomatoes
  • cheese
  • pasta noodles

Instructions

  1. Boil the pasta
  2. Cut the onion and cook with the meat
  3. Add meat and cheese to a mixture with red sauce
  4. pour meat cheese mixture into layers seperated by pasta noodles dividing them evenely
  5. turn the oven on to 400F
  6. Add some cheeese to the top of the lasagna itsself and put in the oven for 40 minutes
