The Worlds Best Mashed Potato Recipe

Description

My father showed me these mashed potatoes growing up and I can never go back

Ingredients

  • Potatoes: I use gold potatoes, however russet works in a pinch
  • 1/2 Stick of butter
  • 3 tbsp cream cheese
  • 5-10 garlic cloves
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup whole milk

Direction

  1. Peel and cut potatoes into even chunks, place the chunks and garlic into a big pot and bring to a boil.
  2. Let the potatoes cook in the boiled water for 20-30 minutes. To check if they are ready stick a fork into the potatoes if its easy to puncture most of the potatoes with little resistance, then they are ready
  3. Optional get an electric mixer, otherwise get a potato mashing device and a large pot. Drain and put the potatoes into the pot along with the butter cream cheese, and milk.
  4. Mash all the ingredients together adding salt and pepper for flavor.
  5. Consume immediately.
